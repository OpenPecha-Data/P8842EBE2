He said, “Can it be, Lord, that there will be be any living beings at a future time who will
hear such dharmas as these being preached and have faith in them?”The Lord said, “Subhūti,
they are not beings, nor are they non-beings.Why is that? ‘All beings,’ Subhūti, have been
preached by the Realized One as beingless. That is why they are called ‘all beings.’
